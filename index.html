Bekri vs Lyoum STARTS ON Tuesday October 4th

A B O U T

Get in the time vehicle to travel with us!

"Bekri vs Lyoum" is a 3-day event dedicated to exhibitions and conferences around 3 themes: programming languages, web development, and game development, as well as a competition of ideas to solve societal problems.

We want this event to allow participants to discover outdated and backward technologies to feel that necessity drives innovation.

Furthermore, encourage innovation and scientific research in the technological field and highlight the ideas.

Indeed, great revolutionary projects started with simple ideas.

In addition, instill in participants the principles of feedback and positive criticism.

IDEATHON

On the 3rd day of the event, participants will choose a societal problem and solve it using technological means and tools and present their solutions in front of a jury that will note the relevance and quality of the solution. The winners will receive free training or a paid Bootcamp.
